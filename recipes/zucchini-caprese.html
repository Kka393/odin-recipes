Zucchini Noodle Caprese

Home

Description

A refreshing twist on Caprese salad, this dish uses spiralized zucchini noodles with creamy mozzarella and juicy tomatoes.

Perfect for warm days and light meals.

Ingredients

  • 4 zucchinis (spiralized)
  • 8 oz cherry tomatoes
  • 8 oz mozzarella balls
  • 1 cup basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp pine nuts
  • ¼ cup cashews
  • ⅓ cup olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Toast pine nuts and cashews.
  2. Blend basil, garlic, lemon juice, nuts, and oil into pesto.
  3. Mix zucchini noodles with tomatoes and mozzarella.
  4. Stir in pesto and serve chilled.
