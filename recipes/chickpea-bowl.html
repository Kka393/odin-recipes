Moroccan Chickpea Bowl

Home

Description

This hearty vegetarian bowl features roasted cauliflower, chickpeas, and onions over spiced rice, topped with yogurt sauce and almonds.

A satisfying fusion of texture and flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • ½ head cauliflower
  • 1 can chickpeas
  • 1 onion
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • Spices: garlic powder, coriander, cinnamon, cloves, ginger
  • 1 cup coconut yogurt
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 cup cilantro
  • ¾ cup pomegranate seeds
  • ¼ cup almonds
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cook rice with spices.
  2. Roast cauliflower, onion, and chickpeas with oil and spices.
  3. Blend yogurt, garlic, and cilantro into sauce.
  4. Assemble bowl with rice, veggies, sauce, and toppings.
  5. Serve warm or chilled.
